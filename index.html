Band Bio

Port Orange’s indie/surf outfit blending three chiming guitars, melodic bass, and energetic drums into a sunny, alt‑surf sound. Formed by five high‑school friends, Inlet writes hook‑driven songs inspired by surf, indie pop, and modern guitar bands.
