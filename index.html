Short Bio

Inlet is an Indie Alt-Rock band formed by five high-school friends from Port Orange, Florida. The quintet loves the beach, skateboarding, and writing catchy, hook-driven songs about love and life. Their sound blends shimmering reverb guitars, tight rhythms, and singable melodies — equal parts sunny surf and indie alt.
