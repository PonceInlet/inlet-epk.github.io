Short Bio

Inlet formed in Port Orange, Florida when five high-school friends turned surf days and skate parks into a band. They write sun-soaked, hook-driven songs about good times, love, and life — blending shimmering reverb and chorus-soaked guitars with tight rhythms and singable melodies. With an unmistakable three-guitar attack, the quintet balances nostalgic surf warmth and contemporary indie edge.
